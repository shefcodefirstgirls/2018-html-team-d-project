Welcome to Bulgaria

Bulgaria is a fantastic holiday destination whether you go in summer or winter alike. Sunny Beach is a popular tourist destination for many brits but if you fancy a less intense party holiday- head inland to pamporvo! Where you can find beautiful walks though desiduous woodland. If you are a thrill seaker then go to pamporovo during winter, where it is a popular destination for skiiers of a whole range of abilities!

A bit of a did you know section?

  • Bulgaria is the only country in Europe which hasn't changed it's name since it was estabilished as a country.
  • Bulgarians shake their head for yes and nod for no.
  • Bulgaria has never lost a single flag in battle.
  • Almost a third of Bulgaria is covered in forest.
  • The Bulgarian Orthodox Church is the oldest Slavic Orthodox Church.
  • The highest mountain in the Balkan Peninsula is in Bulgaria – The Rila Mountain with its highest peak Musala (2,925 m). In Rila are situated the most beautiful group of lakes – The 7 Rila Lakes. Also, there are more than 40 mountains in Bulgaria
